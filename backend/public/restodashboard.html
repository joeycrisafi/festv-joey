About

Estelle is a celebrated Italian restaurant and full-service private event venue. With a dedicated events team, outdoor ceremony terrace, sit-down dining for up to 72 guests, and full bar and AV capabilities, Estelle specializes in weddings, corporate dinners, and milestone celebrations. Kosher meal options available. Minimum spend $18,000 — inclusive of food, bar, staffing, and coordination.
